Privacy Policy

GC Super App — Package: gc.store.app
Effective date: 20 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GC Super App ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile number — required for account creation and OTP-based login.
  • Display name — optional, provided by you from the Profile screen.
  • App usage data — coin balance, transaction history, and coupon validation requests, used to operate the loyalty program.
  • Device push token — if you opt in to notifications, we store an Expo push token to deliver reward and offer alerts.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, precise location, microphone, camera, or any other sensitive device data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you via one-time password (OTP).
  • Display your coin balance, transaction history, and applicable offers.
  • Send transactional and promotional notifications about rewards and offers (only if enabled).
  • Improve app reliability and diagnose technical issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Service providers powering authentication, cloud hosting, and push notifications (e.g., OTP SMS gateway, Railway, Expo).
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law.

4. Data Security

  • All network traffic uses HTTPS (TLS).
  • Authentication tokens are stored using your device's secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain) or browser local storage on web.
  • No passwords are stored — authentication is OTP-based.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 8).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information (e.g., update your display name in-app).
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of promotional notifications from your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

GC Super App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
