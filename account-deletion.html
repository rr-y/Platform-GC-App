Account & Data Deletion

GC Super App — Developer: Rahul Raj Yadav
Package: gc.store.app · Last updated: 20 April 2026

This page explains how users of GC Super App can request deletion of their account and associated personal data, in accordance with the Google Play Data Safety requirements.

Quick summary: email us at rahulrajyadav2022@gmail.com from the mobile number linked to your account (or include it in the message). Your account and personal data are deleted within 7 business days.

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Email (recommended)

  1. Send an email to rahulrajyadav2022@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: Account Deletion Request - GC Super App
  3. In the body, include:
    • The mobile number registered with your account
    • Your display name (if set)
    • Whether you want all data deleted, or only specific data (see below)
  4. We will verify your request by sending an OTP to the registered mobile number and respond within 7 business days.

Option 2 — In-app (coming soon)

A self-service "Delete my account" option will be available in the Profile screen in a future release. Until then, please use the email option above.

What happens when you delete your account

Data that is permanently deleted

  • Your mobile number
  • Your display name
  • Your device push notification token
  • Your authentication records and session tokens

Data that is retained (and for how long)

Data typeRetention periodReason
Coin ledger transactions (anonymised)Up to 7 yearsTax, audit, and accounting compliance under Indian law
Aggregated, non-personal usage statisticsIndefiniteProduct analytics — cannot be linked back to you

Retained data is stripped of personally identifiable information and cannot be traced back to you after deletion.

Partial data deletion

If you only want specific data removed (e.g., your display name or push notification token) while keeping your account active, mention this in your deletion email. We will process partial deletions within the same 7 business-day window.

After deletion

  • You will no longer be able to log in with the deleted mobile number using the existing account.
  • You can create a fresh account anytime by re-registering with the same mobile number, but your previous coin balance, transaction history, and offer redemptions will not be restored.
  • You will stop receiving notifications from GC Super App.
